Instrument View

The upper lane shows the outgoing packet. In reflectron mode, ions enter the ion mirror at the far end, reverse, and travel back on a lower return path into the detector.

Flight Times

The packet width comes from the Gaussian kinetic-energy spread. Reflectron mode adds mirror time so faster ions penetrate deeper before turning and can be refocused near the center energy.
